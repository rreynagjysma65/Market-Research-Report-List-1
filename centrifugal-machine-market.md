<p><p>Centrifugal machines are classified into two main types: continuous and batch. Continuous centrifugal machines operate without interruption, allowing for a steady flow of material, making them ideal for high-volume processing and efficiency. In contrast, batch centrifugal machines process materials in distinct batches, providing flexibility for varying product types and smaller quantities. Each type serves specific industrial applications, with continuous machines favored for mass production and batch machines used for specialty or custom processing needs.</p></p>
