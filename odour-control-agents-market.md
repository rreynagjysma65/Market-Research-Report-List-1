<p><strong>The Odour Control Agents Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Adsorption Control Agent</li><li>Oxidation Control Agent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Odour control agents are crucial in managing undesirable scents across various industries. Adsorption control agents function by trapping odour molecules on their surface, effectively reducing their presence in the environment. Common materials used include activated carbon and zeolites. Oxidation control agents, on the other hand, neutralize odours through chemical reactions, converting odour-causing compounds into less volatile substances. Both types are essential in sectors like waste management, water treatment, and food processing to maintain hygiene and enhance air quality.</p></p>
