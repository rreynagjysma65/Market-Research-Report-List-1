<p><strong>The Coal Gasification Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Moving Bed</li><li>Fluidized Bed</li><li>Entrained Bed</li><li>Molten Bed</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Coal gasification market comprises four primary types: Moving Bed, Fluidized Bed, Entrained Bed, and Molten Bed gasifiers. Moving Bed gasifiers involve the gradual movement of coal down through the reactor, facilitating efficient gas conversion. Fluidized Bed gasifiers use upward gas flow to create a fluid-like state for uniform feed distribution. Entrained Bed gasifiers suspend finely ground coal in a gas stream, promoting rapid reaction. Molten Bed gasifiers operate at high temperatures, using molten materials for heat transfer and reaction facilitation.</p></p>
