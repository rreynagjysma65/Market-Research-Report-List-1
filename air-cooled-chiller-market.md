<p><strong>The Air Cooled Chiller Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air Cooled Scroll Chiller</li><li>Air Cooled Screw Chiller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air-cooled chiller market includes primarily two types: air-cooled scroll chillers and air-cooled screw chillers. Air-cooled scroll chillers are compact, efficient, and suitable for small to medium cooling applications, utilizing a scroll compressor for quiet operation and reliability. In contrast, air-cooled screw chillers are designed for larger applications, featuring twin-screw compressors that offer higher capacity and efficiency, making them ideal for industrial processes or large commercial spaces. Both types utilize ambient air for heat exchange, eliminating the need for cooling towers.</p></p>
