<p><ul><li>Dual Temperature Cold Storage</li><li>Single Temperature Cold Storage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ship Cold Storage System market consists of two main types: Dual Temperature Cold Storage and Single Temperature Cold Storage. Dual Temperature Cold Storage allows for the simultaneous storage of perishable goods at two different temperatures, catering to various products with diverse cooling needs, such as fruits and frozen items. In contrast, Single Temperature Cold Storage maintains a consistent temperature throughout, ideal for storing products that require a uniform climate, like pharmaceuticals or certain food items, ensuring optimal preservation.</p></p>
