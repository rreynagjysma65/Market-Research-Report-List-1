<p><p>Retail casters are essential components in the movement of furniture and equipment, categorized mainly into two types: swivel and rigid casters. Swivel casters allow for multidirectional mobility, making them ideal for maneuverable applications like shopping carts and display racks. In contrast, rigid casters provide straight-line movement and stability, suitable for items that require less frequent directional changes, such as heavy machinery or storage carts. Each type serves specific needs in the retail environment, enhancing efficiency and functionality.</p></p>
