<p><p>The wound tubes and cores market is primarily divided into two types based on their construction: spiral wound tubes and cores, and parallel wound tubes and cores. Spiral wound tubes are created by rotating layers of material around a central axis, allowing for enhanced strength and flexibility, making them ideal for packaging and industrial applications. Parallel wound tubes, on the other hand, feature layers wound parallel to each other, offering consistent thickness and uniformity, suitable for applications requiring precision.</p></p>
