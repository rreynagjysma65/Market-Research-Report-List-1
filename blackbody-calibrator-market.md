<p><p>The blackbody calibrator market is segmented into two primary types: hot plate-type and cavity-type calibrators. Hot plate-type calibrators utilize a heated surface to provide stable and uniform thermal radiation, suitable for various calibration applications. In contrast, cavity-type calibrators feature an enclosed space that achieves high emissivity, providing accurate and consistent temperature measurements. Both types are essential for ensuring the precision of temperature sensing instruments in fields such as scientific research, industrial processes, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
