<p><p>The anti-fog lenses market comprises two main types: physical anti-fog lenses and chemical anti-fog lenses. Physical anti-fog lenses utilize surface treatments that create a hydrophilic layer, allowing moisture to spread evenly, minimizing fog formation. In contrast, chemical anti-fog lenses employ specialized coatings that inhibit fogging through chemical reactions, providing longer-lasting clarity under varying conditions. Both types aim to enhance visibility in environments prone to condensation, catering to industries such as eyewear, safety goggles, and sports gear.</p></p>
