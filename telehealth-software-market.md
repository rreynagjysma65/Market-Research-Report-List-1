<p><p>The telehealth software market encompasses three primary types. Real-time communication enables immediate interactions between patients and healthcare providers via video conferencing or chat, facilitating instant consultations. Store-and-forward allows the transmission of medical data, such as images and lab results, for later review by specialists, aiding in diagnosis without simultaneous interaction. Remote patient monitoring involves gathering patient health data through devices, enabling continuous observation and management of chronic conditions, thus enhancing care and improving outcomes.</p></p>
