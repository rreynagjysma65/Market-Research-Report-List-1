<p><p>The halogen tungsten lamps market is primarily divided into two types: iodine tungsten lamps and bromine tungsten lamps. Iodine tungsten lamps utilize iodine gas to enhance the brightness and lifespan of the bulb, making them energy-efficient and ideal for various applications. In contrast, bromine tungsten lamps use bromine to provide high-intensity light, often found in professional lighting setups. Both types offer superior color rendering, compact sizes, and rapid warm-up times, catering to diverse lighting needs in residential, commercial, and industrial sectors.</p></p>
