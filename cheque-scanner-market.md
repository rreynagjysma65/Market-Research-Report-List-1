<p><p>The cheque scanner market is primarily segmented into two types: single-feed check scanners and multi-feed check scanners. Single-feed check scanners process one check at a time, offering precision and ideal use for low-volume transactions. In contrast, multi-feed check scanners can handle multiple checks simultaneously, making them suitable for high-volume environments, such as banks and retail businesses. Each type caters to specific operational needs, impacting processing speed, efficiency, and overall workflow in cheque handling.</p></p>
