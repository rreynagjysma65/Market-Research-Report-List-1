<p><p>The spiral implant market can be categorized into two main types: conical and cylindrical implants. Conical implants feature a tapered design, promoting enhanced stability and immediate loading capabilities, making them suitable for diverse bone conditions. In contrast, cylindrical implants have a uniform diameter, providing a broader surface area for osseointegration, which is beneficial for patients with varying bone densities. Both types play crucial roles in dental and orthopedic applications, addressing specific clinical needs through their distinct geometries.</p></p>
