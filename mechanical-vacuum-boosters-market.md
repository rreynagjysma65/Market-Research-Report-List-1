Water-ring vacuum boosters utilize water for sealing, offering an environmentally friendly option that's efficient for certain applications. Dry vacuum boosters operate without liquids, making them suitable for clean applications where contamination must be avoided. Each type caters to specific industrial needs and operational requirements.</p></p>
