<p><p>The solar power pump market comprises two main types: DC solar pumps and AC solar pumps. DC solar pumps are directly powered by solar energy, making them efficient for low-power applications, such as irrigation in remote areas. They typically require less maintenance and are easy to install. In contrast, AC solar pumps utilize inverters to convert DC power to AC, allowing for higher power outputs, suitable for larger scale operations, such as agriculture and industrial applications. Both types promote sustainable water management.</p></p>
