<p><ul><li>Resistive Touchscreen Gloves</li><li>Capacitive Touchscreen Gloves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The touchscreen gloves market is primarily divided into two types: resistive and capacitive touchscreen gloves. Resistive touchscreen gloves are designed to work by applying pressure, making them compatible with older touchscreen technology; they often feature a layered design for sensitivity. In contrast, capacitive touchscreen gloves utilize conductive materials that allow for touch sensing by detecting the electrical properties of the skin, making them suitable for modern smartphones and tablets. Both types enhance usability in cold weather while maintaining touch functionality.</p></p>
