<p><p>The IR heaters market is categorized into three main types based on wavelength: short wave (near infrared), medium infrared, and far infrared. Short wave heaters emit high-intensity radiation, providing rapid heating for industrial processes and outdoor applications. Medium infrared offers a balance of heating efficiency and penetration, often used in commercial settings. Far infrared radiates longer wavelengths, delivering gentle warmth ideal for residential use, wellness, and therapeutic applications, making it popular in saunas and personal heating solutions.</p></p>
