<p><ul><li>Single-Tube</li><li>Double Tube</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The damping device market includes two primary types: single-tube and double-tube dampers. Single-tube dampers feature a single cylindrical chamber, providing efficient damping in a compact design, commonly used in lighter vehicles. In contrast, double-tube dampers comprise an inner and outer tube, allowing for enhanced performance under varying conditions, and are often favored in heavy-duty applications. Both types are essential in improving vehicle stability, comfort, and control by absorbing shocks and vibrations during motion.</p></p>
