<p><p>The commercial sweeping machine market consists of two primary types: ride-on sweepers and walk-behind or pedestrian sweepers. Ride-on sweepers are motorized machines designed to accommodate one operator seated on the unit, making them ideal for larger areas that require efficient and fast cleaning. In contrast, walk-behind sweepers are smaller, manually operated devices suited for tighter spaces and urban environments, providing flexibility for detailed cleaning. Both types serve essential roles in maintaining cleanliness in commercial and industrial settings.</p></p>
