<p><p>Voltage mode PWM controllers regulate output voltage by adjusting the duty cycle of the PWM signal. In the full-bridge PWM controller market, both the positive and negative halves of the waveform are utilized, allowing for higher output power and efficiency, commonly used in applications like motor drives and high-performance power supplies. In contrast, half-bridge PWM controllers use only one half of the waveform, making them simpler and more cost-effective for applications with moderate power requirements, such as consumer electronics and small power supplies.</p></p>
