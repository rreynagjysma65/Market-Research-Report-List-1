<p><p>The Alumina Ceramic Flat Sheet Membrane market primarily consists of microfiltration and ultrafiltration types. Microfiltration membranes have larger pore sizes, typically ranging from 0.1 to 10 micrometers, effectively removing suspended solids and larger microorganisms. In contrast, ultrafiltration membranes feature smaller pore sizes, around 0.01 to 0.1 micrometers, enabling the separation of smaller particles, including proteins and colloids. Both types leverage alumina's durability and chemical resistance, making them ideal for various applications in water treatment, food processing, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
