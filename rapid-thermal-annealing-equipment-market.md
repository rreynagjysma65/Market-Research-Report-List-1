<p><p>The Rapid Thermal Annealing Equipment market is segmented into lamp-based and laser-based types. Lamp-based systems utilize high-intensity lamps to heat substrates quickly and uniformly, ideal for semiconductor processing. They offer precise temperature control and fast thermal cycles. In contrast, laser-based systems employ focused laser beams to target specific areas, allowing for localized heating. This method enables fine-tuning of thermal treatment, making it suitable for advanced materials and applications where precision is crucial. Both types enhance processing efficiency and material quality.</p></p>
