<p><ul><li>FC Jumper</li><li>SC Jumper</li><li>ST Jumper</li><li>LC Jumper</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical fiber jumper market includes various types of connectors tailored for specific applications. FC jumpers feature a bayonet-style connector known for its durability and high performance, commonly used in high-precision environments. SC jumpers are popular for their push-pull design, offering ease of use and stability. ST jumpers utilize a bayonet coupling method and are often found in multimode fiber networks. LC jumpers, with their compact design, are ideal for high-density applications, making them widely adopted in data centers.</p></p>
