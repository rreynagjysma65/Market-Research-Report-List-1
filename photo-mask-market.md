<p><p>The photo mask market comprises various types, mainly quartz masks and soda masks. Quartz masks are highly durable and offer superior optical performance, making them ideal for advanced semiconductor manufacturing. They are resistant to thermal and chemical stresses, ensuring longevity and precision in photolithography. On the other hand, soda masks are more cost-effective and suitable for less demanding applications. They possess good optical properties but may not withstand extreme conditions as well as quartz, making them suitable for simpler manufacturing processes.</p></p>
