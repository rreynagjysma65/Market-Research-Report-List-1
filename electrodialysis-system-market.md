<p><ul><li>Continuous Electrodialysis</li><li>Batch Electrodialysis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electrodialysis system market comprises two main types: continuous electrodialysis and batch electrodialysis. Continuous electrodialysis operates in a steady flow, effectively separating ions from solutions in an uninterrupted manner, making it suitable for large-scale applications. In contrast, batch electrodialysis processes the solution in discrete batches, allowing for flexibility and easier control over operating conditions, but is generally used for smaller-scale operations. Both types are utilized in desalination, wastewater treatment, and various industrial applications for ion separation.</p></p>
