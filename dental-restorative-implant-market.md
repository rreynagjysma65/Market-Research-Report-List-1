<p><p>The dental restorative implant market comprises primarily two types: titanium implants and zirconia implants. Titanium implants are widely used due to their strength, biocompatibility, and long-term stability, making them effective for various dental restorations. Zirconia implants, on the other hand, are favored for their aesthetic appeal and ability to blend seamlessly with natural teeth, thus appealing to patients seeking less visible options. Both types offer distinct advantages, catering to diverse patient needs and preferences in dental restoration.</p></p>
