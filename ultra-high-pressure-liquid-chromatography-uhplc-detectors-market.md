<p><p>The Ultra High Pressure Liquid Chromatography (UHPLC) detectors market includes several types. UV-Visible detectors are commonly used for substance quantification based on light absorption. Refractive Index Detectors measure changes in light refraction to identify compounds. Evaporative Light Scattering Detectors (ELSD) detect non-volatile substances, regardless of UV absorption. Fluorescence Detectors offer high sensitivity by measuring light emitted from fluorescent compounds. The "Other" category may include mass spectrometry detectors and other advanced detection technologies, catering to specialized applications.</p></p>
