<p><ul><li>Rolling Spindles</li><li>Air Bearing Spindles</li><li>Liquid Journal Spindles</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high-speed spindle market comprises three main types: rolling spindles, air bearing spindles, and liquid journal spindles. Rolling spindles utilize ball or roller bearings for stability and efficiency, making them suitable for various machining applications. Air bearing spindles eliminate friction by using a thin layer of air, allowing for higher speeds and precision. Liquid journal spindles use a film of liquid to support the spindle, offering smooth operation and reduced wear. Each type serves specific industrial needs with unique advantages.</p></p>
