<p><p>The sludge dewatering equipment market comprises several types, each serving specific needs. Screw presses efficiently reduce sludge volume using a helical screw mechanism. Rotator disc presses utilize rotating discs to separate solids from liquids. Centrifuges employ high-speed rotation for effective solid-liquid separation. Belt filter presses use a moving belt system to dewater sludge through mechanical pressure. Additionally, other market options may include vacuum filters and filtered cake drying systems, catering to various industrial and municipal applications.</p></p>
