<p><ul><li>Rolled Glass Process</li><li>Float Glass Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flat glass for construction market primarily includes two main processes: rolled glass and float glass. The rolled glass process involves heating and rolling molten glass into desired shapes and thicknesses, often resulting in textured surfaces. In contrast, the float glass process produces a smooth, uniform surface by floating molten glass on molten tin, creating clear and high-quality sheets. Both processes cater to diverse applications in architecture and construction, offering options for aesthetic appeal and functional performance in buildings.</p></p>
