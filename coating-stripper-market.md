<p><strong>The Coating Stripper Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>The Caustic Type</li><li>The Acidic Type</li><li>The Solvent Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The coating stripper market comprises three primary types: caustic, acidic, and solvent-based strippers. Caustic types typically use alkaline substances to break down coatings, making them effective for thick paint removal. Acidic strippers, on the other hand, employ acids to dissolve rust and corrosion, working well on metal surfaces. Solvent-based strippers utilize organic solvents to effectively lift and remove coatings, often suitable for a variety of surfaces. Each type serves distinct applications based on the coating material and substrate involved.</p></p>
