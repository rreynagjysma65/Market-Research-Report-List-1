<p><p>The Plastic Smart Cards market is primarily divided into two types: Contact Smart Cards and Contactless Smart Cards. Contact Smart Cards require physical contact with a card reader to transmit data, making them ideal for secure transactions, like banking services. In contrast, Contactless Smart Cards utilize RFID technology, allowing for communication without physical contact, enhancing convenience for applications such as public transportation and mobile payments. Both types cater to diverse industries, emphasizing security, ease of use, and efficient transaction processing.</p></p>
