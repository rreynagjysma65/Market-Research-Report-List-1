<p><p>The blood filtration device market encompasses various types, primarily including hemodialysis membranes, leukocyte filters, and plasma separation membranes. Hemodialysis membranes are designed to remove waste and excess fluids from the blood of patients with kidney failure. Leukocyte filters target the removal of white blood cells to reduce complications in transfusions. Plasma separation membranes are used to isolate plasma components for therapeutic purposes, enhancing treatment options for various medical conditions and improving patient outcomes in clinical settings.</p></p>
