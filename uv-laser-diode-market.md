<p><ul><li>Single Mode</li><li>Multi-mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The UV laser diode market is categorized into two main types: single-mode and multi-mode. Single-mode UV laser diodes offer a narrow beam with low divergence, making them ideal for high-precision applications like microfabrication and sensing. In contrast, multi-mode UV laser diodes emit a broader beam, providing higher power output and efficiency, suitable for applications such as curing and industrial processing. Both types cater to specific needs in sectors like healthcare, electronics, and manufacturing, driving innovation and growth in the market.</p></p>
