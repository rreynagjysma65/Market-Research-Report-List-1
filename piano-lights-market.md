<p><p>Piano lights are categorized into several market types, including tabletop, floor standing, clip-on, and others. Clip-on lights attach easily to the piano, offering portability and convenience for musicians on the go. Other types may include wall-mounted options, providing versatile lighting solutions tailored to various performance and practice environments.</p></p>
