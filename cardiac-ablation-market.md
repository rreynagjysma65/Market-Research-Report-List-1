<p><p>The cardiac ablation market comprises three primary types: electrical cardiac ablation, cryoablation cardiac ablation, and ultrasound cardiac ablation. Electrical cardiac ablation utilizes electrical energy to destroy abnormal heart tissue, optimizing heart rhythm. Cryoablation employs extreme cold to target and eliminate specific heart tissues, offering a less invasive option. Ultrasound cardiac ablation relies on high-frequency sound waves to achieve tissue destruction, providing precision and minimal damage to surrounding areas. Each method serves distinct patient needs in managing cardiac arrhythmias.</p></p>
